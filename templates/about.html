About

NUMY Vote Battle is your ultimate random voting playground where you click what you like most between any two options!

Simple: Two choices → **Click your favorite** → Watch live vote counts update instantly for everyone. Pure fun, no complicated ratings!

Whether it's **pizza vs burger**, **cats vs dogs**, or **any random matchup** – our platform makes voting addictive and shows real-time crowd favorites.

**Zero setup needed**. Persistent storage keeps votes forever.

**Made for fun** by fans, for fans. **Drop specific feedback** to add your dream features!
